About Me

Richard Schiffer Jr. is an American born poker player and skateboarder currently living in Colorado.


Richard (Rich or Filthy Rich) was born in Silicon Valley, California; I guess you could say technology is in his blood! He lived in the south bay area for 20 years before moving out to the Rocky Mountains. In Colorado, he met his beautiful wife, Alyssa, and together they discovered their passion for playing poker. His background for most of his working life has been in sales and customer service. Recently, he as been working in the casino industry as a Table Games Dealer (BlackJack, Roulette, Poker), but is ready to start the next chapter of his life as a developer.


Rich is currently studying Web Development at the University of Denver and his wife is pursuing her nursing degree at the University of Colorado. Upon completion, they plan to take their work on the road and travel the world.
